My Favorite Book : The Golden Compass, by Philip Pullman

Book Summary: Lyra Belacqua lives in a parallel world in which human souls take the form of lifelong animal companions called daemons. Dark forces are at work in the girl's world, and many children have been kidnapped by beings known as Gobblers. Lyra vows to save her best friend, Roger, after he disappears too. She sets out on an epic quest to rescue her friend and save her world.

Main Characters:

Lyra Belacqua- Lyra Belacqua is the lead protagonist and the main character of The Golden Compass. She is an 11-year-old girl who has a best friend named Roger. She's courageous and curious about the world around her. She is cared for her by her uncle, Lord Asriel, and the kindly scholars at Jordan College. When her friend Roger goes missing, she immediatly sets out to find him.

Roger- Roger, Lyra's best friend in Oxford, is a young kitchen boy who's kidnapped by the Gobblers (a gang of people kidnapping young children) in the opening chapters of the novel. Once he disappears, Lyra takes it upon herself to travel to the North to save him.

Lord Asriel- Lord Asriel is one of the antagonists of The Golden Compass - he's a cunning, highly intelligent man of politics. But he's also power-hungry in his quest to rid the universe of Dust, the matter that created the universe.

Ms. Coulter- Ms. Coulter, the second antagonist, lives a glamorous lifestyle travelling the world. She acts as Lyra's original guardian as she travels to find her friend, Roger, but eventually turns against her to prevent her from finding her friend. In the end of the novel, we discover Ms. Coulter is actually Lyra's mother.

Personal Testimony: The Golden Compass is my favorite book because I spent a lot of time reading it as a young child and really enjoyed the book's characters. The twists and turns along the story made me excited to continue reading the book. Additionally, I enjoyed the happy ending of the story.
