Favorite Foods:

My favorite foods are Korean BBQ, tortilla soup, and omelettes. Korean BBQ is marinated steak or chicken meat usually served with vegetables, like tomatoes or onions. It's typical to eat Korean BBQ in a large group, where dishes can be passed around and shared. Tortilla soup is a spicy broth that can be served with chicken ,tomatoes, beans, and fried onions. Omlettes are simple and easy to make when in a rush - they're eggs stuffed with cheese, ham, tomatoes, and other vegetables. Recipes are below:
